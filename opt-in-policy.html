SMS Authentication Messaging Policy

Last Updated: Nov 1, 2024

Overview

Lining Link LLC uses SMS text messaging solely for account security and authentication purposes, including password reset verification codes. This document outlines our SMS messaging terms and conditions.

Message Purpose

Our SMS messages are limited to:

  • Password reset verification codes
  • Account security notifications
  • Authentication codes
  • Account access verification

Consent

By initiating a password reset or requesting a verification code, you consent to receive a one-time SMS message containing your security code. This consent applies only to the specific security request you initiate.

Message Frequency

  • Messages are sent only when you request a password reset or authentication code
  • One message per request
  • Messages are transactional and not promotional in nature

Message and Data Rates

  • Standard message and data rates may apply
  • Contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan
  • Lining Link LLC does not charge for these security messages

Supported Carriers

Our authentication messages work with all major wireless carriers, including:

  • AT&T
  • T-Mobile
  • Verizon
  • Sprint

Security Information

  • Never share your verification code with anyone
  • Our representatives will never ask for your verification code
  • Verification codes expire after 10 minutes
  • Each code is unique and can only be used once

Privacy & Data Protection

  • Your phone number is used exclusively for account security purposes
  • We do not use your phone number for marketing or promotional purposes
  • Your phone number is stored securely and handled according to our Privacy Policy at https://lininglink.com/legal.html
  • We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties

Technical Support

If you don't receive your verification code:

  • Check that your provided phone number is correct
  • Ensure your phone can receive SMS messages
  • Contact support at: admin@lininglink.com

Contact Us

For issues with authentication messages:

  • Email: admin@lininglink.com
  • Support Hours: 9am-5pm, Mon-Fri

Compliance

This service follows:

  • Industry security standards
  • Data protection regulations
  • CTIA Guidelines
  • Carrier Requirements

Service Availability

Message delivery depends on your wireless carrier's coverage and service availability. Lining Link LLC is not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
